Support

Welcome to the Ninety9 support page. Below you'll find answers to common questions about the app.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. Ninety9 works entirely offline. All names, audio, and content are stored on your device.

No sound is playing — what should I check?

Make sure your device is not on silent mode and the volume is turned up. You can also try switching ambient sounds using the sound icon in the top bar, or selecting "Silent" and switching back to a sound.

How do I change the ambient sound?

Tap the sound icon in the top-right corner of any screen. You can choose from Rain, Waves, Thunderstorm, Birds, or Silent.

How does the quiz work?

The quiz displays one of the 99 names and asks you to identify the correct meaning from three choices. Tap a choice, then tap Submit. You'll see immediate feedback — gold for correct, red for incorrect. Tap Next to continue.

Can I use the app to study in both Arabic and English?

Yes. From the home screen choose Arabic → English to see Arabic names and guess the English meaning, or English → Arabic to do the reverse. The quiz also supports both directions.

How do I shuffle the names?

In the study screen, tap the shuffle icon (⇄) in the top-right corner. Tap it again to return to the original order.
